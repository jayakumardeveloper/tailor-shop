Our Story

A neighbourhood shop with an uncompromising eye

SEENU Tailor Shop sits on the Market Line of Thennasalai Road in Velrampet, Puducherry. We're deliberately small — one shop, one experienced hand, and a belief that fit is everything.

Chudithars are our speciality, but we stitch all types of dresses — everyday wear, office wear, and special-occasion outfits. Every garment is measured, cut, and sewn right here in the shop.

  • Measurements taken in person for a perfect fit
  • Chudithars, blouses, salwars, and all types of ladies wear
  • Alterations and re-fitting handled in-house
